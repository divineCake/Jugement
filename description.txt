Judgement is a mod planned to be a huge content adding mod!

Current content:
2 new types of pre-hardmode ore, which requires a crimtane/corruption pick or higher to mine.
Nanestine ore: used to create nanestine armor for range and melee, and also tools.
Valiotite ore: used to create Valiotite armor for mage and summoner, and also tools.

Both ores glows in the dark, Nanestine glowing green and Valiotite glowing purple.
